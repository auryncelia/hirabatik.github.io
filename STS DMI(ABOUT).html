About Us

Hira Batik (हीर/Heer; means diamond) is a luxury brand that specializes in traditional Indonesian clothing. This brand has a mission that is to preserve traditional craftsmanship and make it accessible to all. We believe that all should be able to wear their culture and heritage on their sleeve, without going over top or looking like they're trying too hard.

Hira Batik designs are about being comfortable and beautiful,but still stylish; they're about honoring your own unique style while also looking fashionable. Our goal is to empower people around the world with the knowledge that their own style is celebrated—not judged—and we hope you'll join us in our mission!
